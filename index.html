Welcome to UpTime

UpTime is the best way to explore the web at warp speed. Tab, swipe, and double tap to browse faster than ever before. UpTime is kind of like other web browsers but with a few tricks up its sleeve.

1) Quickly open new tabs in the background

Remember tapping and holding on a link and waiting… … … for the menu to popup with the Open in New Page button? Forget that. Instead double tap the “Add Tab” button + to open every link in a new tab in the background.

2) Fly through the web with keyboard shortcuts

Uptime includes over 50 familiar keyboard shortcuts. So pair your external keyboard and try a few out.

Also use ⌘1 to ⌘9 while on a search results page to quickly open the corresponding result.
